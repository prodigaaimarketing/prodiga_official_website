AI Marketing & Automation
Questions about our marketing services, automation, and what results to expect.
What is AI marketing and how is it different from regular marketing? +

Traditional marketing is manual — someone writes a post, someone manages ads, someone follows up on leads. AI marketing automates all of this intelligently. Key differences:

  • AI chatbots respond to leads in 2 seconds instead of 47 hours
  • AI voice agents call 1,000+ prospects a day without human staff
  • AI ad targeting continuously optimizes itself to reduce your cost-per-lead
  • Automated WhatsApp sequences nurture leads while you sleep

The result: more leads, lower costs, and a system that works 24/7 without human intervention.

How long before I see results from AI marketing? +

It depends on the service:

  • Meta/Google Ads: First leads typically within 3–7 days of launch
  • WhatsApp Automation: Active within 7–14 days of setup
  • AI Chatbot: Live and responding within 10–14 days
  • SEO: Meaningful organic traffic growth in 60–90 days
  • Full funnel optimization: Best results at the 60–90 day mark

We guarantee results conversations — you'll always know exactly what's working and what we're doing to improve it.

Do I need a big budget for Meta/Google ads? +

No. We've seen excellent results with ad budgets starting from ₹15,000/month. The key isn't the budget size — it's how well the targeting, creative, and funnel are built. A ₹15,000 well-optimised campaign will consistently outperform a ₹60,000 poorly run campaign.

We'll recommend a budget based on your industry, target area, and goals during the Strategy Call.

What is a WhatsApp Voice Agent and how does it work? +

An AI Voice Agent is software that makes real phone calls to your leads — in a natural, human-sounding voice — to qualify them, answer questions, and schedule appointments. Here's how it works:

  • A new lead comes in via your website, Meta ads, or WhatsApp
  • The AI Voice Agent calls them within minutes (not hours)
  • It has a natural conversation, asks qualifying questions
  • If qualified, it books them into your calendar automatically
  • Full transcript and CRM update happens instantly

Will the AI chatbot really replace my customer support team? +

For most common queries — yes. In our experience, a well-built AI chatbot handles 85–94% of incoming questions without any human involvement. This includes FAQs, pricing inquiries, appointment booking, order status, and lead qualification.

The remaining 5–15% of complex queries get seamlessly handed off to a human agent. Your team stops answering the same questions 50 times a day and focuses on high-value conversations that actually move deals forward.

Do you manage ads on an ongoing basis or just set them up? +

We offer both:

  • Full management (retainer): We run, optimize, and report on your campaigns every month. You get weekly performance updates and monthly strategy reviews.
  • Setup + handover: We build everything, train your team, and hand over control. Best for businesses with an in-house marketer.

Most clients choose full management because ongoing optimization makes a huge difference to cost-per-lead over time.

How long does it take to build an app or website? +

Depends on the complexity:

100% yes. Once payment is complete, you own everything — the source code, the design files, the database schemas, all assets. We transfer the complete codebase to your GitHub repository and you can take it to any developer in the future.

What is the payment structure for software projects? +

For software development, we follow a milestone-based payment structure:

  • 30% upfront at project kickoff
  • 30% at design approval (before coding starts)
  • 30% at beta delivery (live, working version)
  • 10% at final handover + source code transfer

You only pay for work that's been done and approved. No money changes hands for future work until the current milestone is complete.

Is there a minimum contract period for marketing retainers? +

We recommend a minimum of 3 months for marketing retainers. Here's why: most AI marketing systems take 30–60 days to properly set up, test, and optimize. Judging results in the first month is like giving up on a gym membership after 2 workouts.

Our contracts are structured as 3-month initial terms, then month-to-month. If after 3 months you're not seeing measurable improvement, we'll work with you to fix it or give you a clear plan — no lock-in beyond that.

Working with Prodiga

What information do I need to provide to get started? +

For marketing, we typically need:

  • Access to your existing ad accounts (Meta Business Manager, Google Ads)
  • Brand assets (logo, colors, any existing creatives)
  • Your target customer profile and geography
  • Your monthly budget (for ad spend)

For software projects, we need your product requirements, any wireframes or references you have, and your preferred tech preferences (if any). We guide you through everything in the discovery session — you don't need to have everything ready beforehand.

How much of my time will be required? +

We've specifically designed our process to require minimum time from our clients:

  • Onboarding: 2–4 hours in the first week (one-time)
  • Weekly check-ins: 30 minutes (optional, but recommended)
  • Approvals: We send you things to approve; you respond when convenient
  • Monthly review: 60 minutes

Total: roughly 3–5 hours per month after the initial setup. Most clients tell us this is the least hands-on agency relationship they've ever had.

What if I'm not happy with the work? +

We have a structured feedback and revision process. For marketing, every strategy and creative goes through your approval before going live. For software, you approve every design before coding begins. We don't move to the next phase without your sign-off.

If there's a genuine quality issue that we haven't been able to resolve after good-faith efforts, please refer to our Refund Policy. We always aim to resolve issues — refunds are the last resort, not the first.

Do you work on weekends and public holidays? +

Our standard working hours are Monday–Saturday, 10am–7pm IST. For urgent issues (ad campaigns going down, critical bugs), we have an emergency WhatsApp channel that's monitored 7 days a week.
