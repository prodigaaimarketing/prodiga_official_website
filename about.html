Our Story

Why we built Prodiga

Prodiga AI Infinity Ventures was founded on a simple observation — growing businesses were hiring a marketing freelancer, a software developer and a course platform separately, and nothing connected. Strategy lived in one place, execution in another, and results suffered in between.

We built Prodiga as a single growth partner instead — AI marketing & automation, app & software development, and AI-led learning, working from one team, one roadmap and one shared understanding of the business.

Today, Prodiga AI Infinity Ventures — powered by Skybull E Learning (OPC) Pvt. Ltd. — works with real estate brands, healthcare professionals, coaches and startup founders across West Bengal & Tripura, with the systems built to scale well beyond.

Our Mission

To give growth-focused businesses one accountable team for AI marketing, automation and software — so they stop juggling vendors and start compounding results.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted AI growth partner for real estate, healthcare, coaching and startup businesses across East India — and build the systems and talent that take them beyond it.
