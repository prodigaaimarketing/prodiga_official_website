About Us

Dhubulia's Most Trusted Second-Hand Vehicle Dealer

At Bike & Scooter — DS Automobiles Dealer, we believe everyone deserves reliable, affordable personal transport. Since our founding, we've been helping families, students, and professionals across Dhubulia and nearby areas find the perfect pre-owned bike or scooter.

Located conveniently on NH 12, Dhubulia, we stock a wide range of verified bikes and scooties from trusted brands. Our team personally inspects each vehicle for engine health, body condition, and proper documentation before it reaches our showroom floor.

We're not just selling vehicles — we handle RC transfer, insurance renewal, and finance paperwork so your purchase is smooth from first inquiry to final handover.
