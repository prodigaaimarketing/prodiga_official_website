Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need To Know
Before You Start.

What makes Prodiga different from other digital agencies?
Prodiga is India's first AI-first business growth company. Unlike traditional agencies, every Prodiga service is powered by cutting-edge AI tools — making delivery faster, more accurate, and far more cost-effective. We combine AI marketing + apps + software under one roof with transparent pricing.
How quickly can I expect to see results?
Most clients see measurable improvements within 30–60 days. Lead generation and ad campaigns often show results within the first 2 weeks. SEO takes 90–120 days for significant ranking improvement. AI automation results are immediate from Day 1.
Do I need technical knowledge to use your services?
Absolutely not. We handle everything from strategy to implementation to maintenance. You only need to provide business information and approve work. Our team manages all technical aspects and also provides training sessions.
Are there any hidden charges beyond the listed pricing?
Never. All Prodiga pricing is fully transparent. The only additional costs are third-party tools (like WhatsApp Business API, ad spend budgets, or cloud hosting fees) which are always disclosed upfront before you commit.
What is the minimum contract period for monthly services?
We recommend a minimum 3-month commitment for marketing services to see meaningful results, especially SEO and organic growth. However, we don't lock you into long contracts — we earn your business every single month.
Do you work with businesses outside India?
Yes. While headquartered in West Bengal, India, we serve clients across India, Bangladesh, UAE, UK, USA, and Australia. All services are fully deliverable remotely. We accept both INR and international payments.
What AI tools and platforms do you use?
We use an extensive AI stack including: ChatGPT-4 / Claude for content, n8n / Make for automation, GoHighLevel for CRM, Vapi / ElevenLabs for voice AI, Tidio / Botpress for chatbots, Jasper for marketing copy, MidJourney for visuals, and custom-built tools per client need.
How does the AI WhatsApp automation work?
We set up the official WhatsApp Business API connected to an AI model trained on your business. It handles customer queries, sends follow-ups, qualifies leads, sends broadcast campaigns, and routes hot leads to your team — all automatically, 24/7.
Can you integrate with our existing CRM or tools?
Yes. We integrate with 200+ tools including Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, Freshworks, Google Workspace, Notion, Slack, Razorpay, Shopify, WooCommerce, and more. Custom API integrations are also available for proprietary systems.
How do you ensure data security and privacy?
All client data is handled with strict confidentiality. We sign NDAs for enterprise projects, use encrypted storage, maintain separate data environments per client, and comply with Indian IT Act and GDPR guidelines. Your business data is never shared.
Do you offer white-label or agency partnership services?
Yes! We have a dedicated white-label program for marketing agencies and consultants who want to resell our AI services under their brand. This includes white-labelled reports, client dashboards, and deliverables. Contact us for agency partnership pricing.
What happens if I'm not satisfied with the results?
We have a clear performance accountability process. For marketing services, we set agreed KPIs upfront and provide transparent monthly reporting. If we consistently miss targets, we offer service credits, strategy revision sessions, and in rare cases, prorated refunds.
How long does website or app development take?
Business websites: 7–10 days. Corporate websites: 14–21 days. E-commerce: 21–30 days. Landing pages: 3–5 days. Android/iOS apps: 6–12 weeks. SaaS platforms: 8–16 weeks. Custom ERP/CRM: 12–20 weeks. All timelines assume timely client feedback.
Do you provide ongoing support after project delivery?
Yes. All development projects include 3 months of free support for bug fixes and minor updates. After that, we offer affordable monthly maintenance retainers. For marketing services, support is ongoing as part of the monthly retainer.
Can I start with a small budget and scale up?
Absolutely. Our Starter Package begins at just ₹15,000/month. Many clients start with one or two services and add more as they see results. Start small, prove ROI, then scale confidently — with no pressure to commit to large packages immediately.
What ad budget do I need for Meta/Google Ads?
Our management fee is separate from your ad spend budget. For Meta Ads, we recommend minimum ₹10,000–₹15,000/month ad spend. For Google Ads, ₹15,000–₹25,000/month minimum. These budgets go directly to Meta/Google — not to Prodiga.
Do you offer industry-specific AI solutions?
Yes. We have specialized solution stacks for Healthcare, Real Estate, Education, Restaurants, Retail, Manufacturing, and Professional Services. Each is pre-configured with the tools, templates, and automations that work best for that sector.
How is payment structured for development projects?
For development projects: 50% upfront, 25% at midpoint delivery, 25% on final delivery. For monthly services: first month paid upfront, then monthly recurring. All payments processed securely via Razorpay with proper invoices.
Is the Infinity Package customizable?
